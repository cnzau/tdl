Our Platform

NeuInvest is a payment solution developed by a team of diverse business leaders and IT gurus/professionals.

In the recent past, clients didn’t have a constant systematic way of ensuring their payments are done in an effective and timely manner.

Using a simple USSD or Web Application, clients are able to submit payments, track balances in addition to their payments being remitted directly to the seller(merchants) account.

We all have things that we want to achieve but lack the means or knowhow on how to go about it. NeuInvest platform makes it possible for millions of Kenyans to achieve their aspirations and dreams through an automated and structured payment plan.
