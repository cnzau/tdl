#301 (Permanent) Redirect: Point an entire site to a different URL on a permanent basis. This is the most common type of redirect and is useful in most situations. Here we are redirecting to the "neuinvest.com" domain:

# This allows you to redirect your entire website to any other domain
#Redirect 301 / http://neuinvest.com/

#302 (Temporary) Redirect: Point an entire site to a different temporary URL. This is useful for SEO purposes when you have a temporary landing page and plan to switch back to your main landing page at a later date:

# This allows you to redirect your entire website to any other domain
Redirect 302 / http://neuinvest.com/
